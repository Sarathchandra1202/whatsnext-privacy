Privacy Policy

We collect only the data necessary to provide the service:

  • Email address — for authentication only
  • Username & country/region — to personalise your experience
  • Movie ratings and watchlist — to power recommendations
  • Comments and community chat messages — displayed in-app

We do not sell, share, or monetise your personal data. Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen inside the app.

For privacy enquiries contact: privacy@whatsnextmovie.app

Data Deletion

Profile → Settings (⚙) → Delete Account

Your account will be deactivated immediately and all data will be permanently deleted after 30 days. To cancel a deletion request, simply sign back in within the 30-day window and choose "Restore Account".

Automated Data Cleanup

To keep the app running efficiently, we run automated background jobs (cron jobs) that periodically clean up the following data:

  • Community chat messages — messages older than 30 days are permanently deleted. Movie-specific comments are kept indefinitely.
  • Deleted accounts — when you request account deletion, your account is deactivated immediately. All your data (ratings, watchlist, comments) is permanently deleted after a 30-day grace period. You can restore your account at any time within this window by signing back in.

Chat Monitoring & Encryption

⚠️ Community chat and group messages are NOT end-to-end encrypted.

Messages are stored on our servers and may be reviewed by us for the purposes of safety moderation, abuse investigation, and compliance with applicable law.

Do not share sensitive personal information, passwords, financial details, or any private information in chat.

By using the chat features you acknowledge and consent to this monitoring.

Community & Age Safety

This app is available to users aged 13 and over. Users under 18 should have parental or guardian consent before creating an account.

The in-app community chat and group messaging features allow users to post messages that may be seen by others. We moderate community content and automatically filter profanity and slurs. Despite this, we cannot guarantee that all user-generated content will be appropriate for younger audiences at all times.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise younger users when using chat features. If you encounter content that is inappropriate or harmful, please contact us so we can take action.

Users must not post content that is sexually explicit, violent, hateful, or otherwise harmful. Accounts found violating these rules will be removed.
